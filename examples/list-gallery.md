`Concept 01 · Editorial`

## Trust the typography. No card chrome, just hanging numerals and body text.

---

<!-- _class: list-editorial -->
<!-- _footer: "Editorial · list-editorial" -->

## What changed since the last review.

1. Net revenue retention climbed nine points after the price reset.
2. Two of three pilot accounts converted to annual contracts.
3. The platform engineering team consolidated under a single VP.
4. Compliance certifications closed on schedule for SOC 2 and HIPAA.

---

<!-- _class: list-editorial lede -->
<!-- _footer: "Editorial with lede · list-editorial lede" -->

## Three convictions for the year ahead.

1. **The pricing experiment is not a one-time correction.** We expect to run two more structured price tests in the second half, each gated by a churn-impact threshold.
2. **Sales-led growth is the bottleneck, not the strategy.** The pipeline is healthy at the top — the friction is in deal mechanics, not in demand.
3. **The platform team is the highest-leverage hire we make this year.** Every product bet downstream of it accelerates or stalls on its capacity.

`Concept 03 · Spectrum`

## The lattice signature gradient runs vertically as a rail. The signature shows up inside content, not just at the slide edge.

## What the market is telling us.

- Buyers want consolidated tooling — point solutions are out of favor.
- Procurement cycles have lengthened by roughly six weeks since last year.
- Three competitors have repositioned away from mid-market in the last quarter.
- Security review is now the longest stage in every enterprise deal we close.

---

<!-- _class: list-spectrum sequence -->
<!-- _footer: "Spectrum sequence · list-spectrum sequence" -->

1. Internal dogfood — engineering and platform teams only.

3. Open beta — public waitlist with managed onboarding.
4. General availability — self-serve and assisted tiers.
