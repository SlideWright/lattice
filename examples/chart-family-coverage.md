# More charts join the family

`Chart family · Coverage`

`journey`, `word-cloud`, and `roadmap` now render in the shared chart-frame skeleton — the same header, caption, and surface every other chart already wears — and roadmap's state markers go colour-blind-safe.

---

<!-- _class: content -->
<!-- _footer: "Why fold them in · content" -->

## One family, defined by the frame — not the bucket.

A chart-family member is any layout the engine wraps in `.chart-frame`. That net is wider than "data series": `state-chart` is a graph, `journey` is a structured walk, `word-cloud` is a packed canvas. What they now share is the skeleton — eyebrow, centered title, body, caption — and the opt-in `canvas` surface panel. `journey` and `word-cloud` were the last two charts mirroring that chrome by hand; they now inherit the real thing.
