# The Global South, first-class

`Evidence · Spatial · Series`

A stated, dated definition that ships with the data — pinned to the UN Group of 77 + China, dated 2025.

---

<!-- _class: content -->
<!-- _footer: "Why ship it" -->

## Omission isn't neutral.

Leaving "Global South" out doesn't keep the engine impartial — it just makes every deck hand-roll an undocumented ~130-country roster, so the political call lands invisibly and inconsistently. Shipping **one sourced, dated roster** is the transparent choice: the definition travels with the data, and an author can cite it.
