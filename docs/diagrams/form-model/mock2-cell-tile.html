Cell → Tile

A Cell is a typed slot — a sized, positioned region of the slide (the old word was berth). A Tile is the leaf that fills one Cell, on a z-plane. Cell = where; Tile = what.

Read it as: Cells are the typed slots (masthead-lede, masthead-bay, stage, footer-left, progress-centre, pagination-right…); each Tile fills one Cell and stacks by z (flattens to z-index in 2D; literal depth in a spatial renderer). The author's title+kicker lift into the masthead-lede, meta docks in the masthead-bay, the content Tile (z2) fills the stage, and the footer-row Cells take footer/progress/pagination.
