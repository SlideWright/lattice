## Revenue forecasting just shifted from gut feel to a system of record.

For a decade, the forecast was a spreadsheet ritual — reps sandbagging, managers second-guessing, a number nobody trusted. AI-driven deal signals have ended that era. The forecast is now something you can measure, audit, and improve.

- The shift: from manager intuition to evidence drawn from the deals themselves.
- Teams still running the old ritual are forecasting blind against teams that aren't.
